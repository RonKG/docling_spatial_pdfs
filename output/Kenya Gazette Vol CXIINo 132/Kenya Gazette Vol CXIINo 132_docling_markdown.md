## MINISTRY OF STATE FOR IMMIGRATION AND REGISTRATION OF PERSONS

## ESTABLISHMENT OF THE TASK FORCE ON CITIZENSHIP AND RELATED PROVISIONS OF THE CONSTITUTION

IT  IS  notified  for  the  general  information  of  the  public  that  the Minister of State for Immigration and Registration of Persons, for the purposes  of developing  legislation on citizenship  and  compiling proposals for amendments in the existing related Acts of Parliament in fulfilment of the requirements of Chapter Three and Fifth Schedule of the  Constitution  on  enactment  of  legislations,  hereby  establishes  a Task Force.

1. The Task Force shall comprise of the following persons:

Mumo Matemu -(Chairperson) James Omondi Opundo -(Vice-Chairperson)

## Members

Ruth Bolline Aluoch Caroline Achieng Oduor Margaret Chepng'etich Lucy Njeri Njihia Stephen Kokonya John Muiwa Kinyumu Omar Abdallar Dhadho Josephine Sinyo Paul Dume Mwangemi Arun Devani Lucy Wangari Karanja

## Joint Secretaries:

Caroline Oshuru Oyula Nicholas Ongeri Maronda Susan Nyawira Kuruga

2. The Terms of Reference of the Task Force are to:
2. (a) Prepare a detailed work plan indicating the detailed milestones and timelines and present to the appointing authority.

3. In carrying out its mandate, the Task Force shall be answerable to the Minister of State for Immigration and Registration of Persons through the Permanent Secretary.
4. The Task Force Secretariat shall be based at Maendeleo House, 7th Floor, P.O. Box 30395-00100, Nairobi, Kenya.
5. The terms of office of the Task Force shall be for a period of nine (9)  months  with  effect  from  16th  December,  2010,  and  the

Minister of State for Immigration and Registration of Persons may, when necessary, extend the period.

Dated the 16th December, 2010.

OTIENO KAJWANG',

Minister of State for Immigration and Registration of Persons.
