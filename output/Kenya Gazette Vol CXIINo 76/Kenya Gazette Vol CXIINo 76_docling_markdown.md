## THE CONSTITUTION OF KENYA (REVIEW) REFERENDUM REGULATIONS, 2010

TRANSFER OF POLLING STATION

IN EXERCISE of powers conferred by Regulation 6 (1) ( d ) of the National Assembly and Presidential Elections (Registration of

Electors) Regulations, section 38 of the Constitution of Kenya Review Act and regulation 21 (2) ( a )  of  the  Constitution  of Kenya  (Review) Referendum Regulations, the Interim Independent Electoral Commission notifies the voters of Mukuru Kaiyaba Primary School in Makadara Constituency; Code 001, Nairobi that the polling centre has been transferred to Bright Star Academy .

AND THAT the reason(s) for the transfer is (are) as follows:

Mukuru  Kaiyaba  Primary  School  is  under  construction  and  the contractor  has  denied  access  for  polling  day  activities  for  fear  of destruction of the property in the likelihood of poll violence.

Dated the 2nd August, 2010.

## A. I. HASSAN,

Chairman,

Interim Independent Electoral Commission.
