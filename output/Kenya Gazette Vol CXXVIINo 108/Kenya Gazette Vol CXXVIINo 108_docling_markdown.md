WHEREAS  Gazette  Notice  No.  5971  of  2024,  published  the  water  and  sanitation  tariffs  for  Gatanga  Water  and  Sanitation  Company (GATAWASCO);

And whereas, upon further review and consideration of Murang'a County Government subsidy to GATAWASCO, it has been determined that the tariffs need to be revised downwards;

Now, therefore, pursuant to section 72 (1) (b) and (2) of the Water Act 2016, the Water Services Regulatory Board revokes the tariff structure published in the Gazette Notice No. 5971 of 2024.

Consequently, it is notified that the revised water and sanitation tariffs for GATAWASCO for the period 2024/2025 to 2027/2028 shall be as follows:

- 1.2 Sewerage Tariff for the period 2024/2025 to 2027/2028
