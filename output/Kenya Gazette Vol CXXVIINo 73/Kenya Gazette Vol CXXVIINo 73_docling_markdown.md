## THE PETROLEUM ACT

( Cap. 308)

CONSTITUTION OF PETROLEUM BLOCKS

IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 15 (1) of the Petroleum Act, the Cabinet Secretary for Energy and Petroleum in consultation with the Advisory Committee constitutes the petroleum blocks as set out in the First Schedule and as further specified in the geographical coordinates in the respective basin block maps in the Second Schedule consisting of, six blocks located in the Anza Basin, twenty-nine blocks located in the Lamu Basin, three blocks in the Mandera Basin and twelve blocks located in the Tertiary Rift Basin. Gazette Notice No. 3344 of 2016 is revoked.
